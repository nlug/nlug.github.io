Norrköping Linux User Group

The primary purpose of Norrköping Linux User Group (NLUG) is to create and maintain a social space where both Linux aficionados as well as complete newcomers can meet up, learn, share ideas and collaborate together.

Topics for discussions can be such as: GNU/Linux, DevOps, Kernel hacking, System Administration, Open-Source development, Docker and containers, Raspberry Pi, hardware and anything in between.

Meetings

This group was just recently founded but already got around twenty people who emailed interest to join. Unfortunately Covid-19 happened, so we had to cancel our first "Linux Mingel" that was planned earlier. We will announce a new meetup date as soon as possible in our Slack channel!
